My Lovely Camera
January 4, 2026

Time for post #2. I made a new year's resolution to try and make a post every week so here it is for week 1. With new year celebrations finishing and my friends starting to go back to school or traveling away, I wanted to talk about something that has become a big hobby in my life. This hobby started on my 19th birthday when I got my first camera, a canon eos r50. This camera has been amazing, and with the two other lenses I've gotten since then, I have been able to really experiment with composition and expand beyond simple portrait photography. Over this almost year and a half journey I’ve really enjoyed being able to do things in nature and subsequently capture these beautiful moments. And even though I haven’t been to as many places, the beautiful thing about photography is that location or subject doesn’t really define a good photographer, rather what someone chooses to do with what is available to them. This idea can be taken in the sense of not letting your equipment define your ceiling of photo quality, but more in my case, the environment around me. Since winter break I haven’t had the chance to really explore outside my home town but I still have taken some pretty cool pictures I wanted to share.
