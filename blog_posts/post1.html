And now to the present month. I just finished school like last thursday and only really came back yesterday so this is super fresh in my mind. The 2nd and 3rd week of december was probably the most locked in period of the whole semester. These two weeks I had my nightmare of a 364 project due (buzzer beater finish), 301 project (not bad), but then just like all my finals and doing basically half a semester’s worth of work for our SIF pitch all in like one day. I want to talk more about the SIF pitch. We pitched a really cool company and I did a lot of research for them throughout the semester. However, I didn’t work as much on the model as I should have been and I could’ve been more proactive in asking for help with our PM for the revenue projections as that was basically the biggest bottleneck. Well anyways, I ended up working on the model and pitch report from 10 AM friday to 5 AM saturday with our pitch being at like 11 AM that same saturday. It was super stressful as there were times I didn’t think I could do it and honestly, I was really scared of making a fool of myself. I really care about the work I do at SIF and was disappointed that I waited so long to do a bulk of the work. I also found out at like 1 AM that we were going to be one of the groups who presented in front of the dean. That actually had me geeked. I respect aayush a lot and he was honestly a great pres. I answered all the follow-up questions well and I was really proud of my work. All I think about it now was imagine how much better we could have done if we stuck to a better schedule. While everyone always says “this is just how these things are” I don’t think it has to be this way. Best believe next semester my pitch will be even better with more detail and presented better.
