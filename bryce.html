Bryce Canyon National Park
Utah's incredible amphitheater of hoodoos

Bryce Canyon is famous for its thousands of spire-shaped rock formations called hoodoos. I visited in Spring Break '25 and was one of the two parks I saw on my trip to Utah -> Zion up next!
