HealthEquity (HQY)
Dec 12, 2025  •  Equity Research  •  Ticker: HQY
HealthEquity (HQY) is a BUY as the clear market leader in the structurally growing HSA industry, benefiting from powerful secular tailwinds such as rising healthcare costs, increasing adoption of high-deductible health plans, and favorable legislation. Its high-retention (~97%), platform-based model generates recurring, high-margin revenue that compounds as HSA balances grow and earn higher yields over time. Deeply integrated technology, scale advantages, and high switching costs create durable competitive moats, supporting sustained share gains and long-term cash flow growth. The investment thesis would be challenged by a prolonged economic slowdown that reduces employer benefits adoption, a faster-than-expected decline in interest rates that compresses custodial yield revenue, heightened competitive pricing pressure from large payroll or fintech platforms, or adverse regulatory changes that weaken HSA tax advantages or raise compliance costs.
